# CHANGELOG — v15.5.26

## Changed — Big & Clear mode is now ON by default
The Classic register now starts in **Big & Clear** (high-contrast,
large-text) mode out of the box, for maximum readability at the counter.

- A cashier can still turn it off (the ♿ "Big & Clear" button or Alt+A);
  that choice is remembered per device and respected on next load.
- Voice announcements remain **off** by default (opt-in via the 🔊 button
  or Alt+V), so checkouts aren't noisy unless wanted.
- Screen-reader announcements (aria-live) and audio beeps are always on.
